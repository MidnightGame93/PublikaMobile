Feeling bored? Look no further. Keep yourself occupied and attend to our latest events and exhibitions held here in Publika!


Makanlah Buah-Buahan Tempatan Exhibition

We are so excited by the upcoming Makanlah Buah-Buahan Tempatan Exhibition '13, starting from July 12th - July 14th. Be sure to mark your calenders, because it's going to be a fruitiliciously sweet exhibit that you wouldn't want to miss! To know more about this exhibition, click here!


Future Traditions by Dreamlab

Have you been to any of the events on schedule at Future Traditions by Dreamlab? Hop on down to Art Row and check them out! Just click here to find out more!


New Malay Literature:The Changing Face of The Malay Author

Join us at Black Box@Publika on June 22nd for a session featuring non-Malay writers who write literature in Bahasa Malaysia. Admission is free, but space is limited - so hurry!


Storytelling in Bahasa Malaysia with Mama Tok

Don't you just love a good storytelling session? Bring your kids to enjoy stories in Bahasa Malaysia with Mama Tok on 23 June at White Box@Publika!


Genesis 1005 Design and Illustration Exhibition

Come join our friends from Genesis 1005 for their "GENESIS 1005 | Design & Illustration Exhibition", held from the 18th till the 22nd of May at White Box, Publika. We are simply eager to delve into the works of creativity produced by these talented young artists, and we hope you feel as much as we do too! Here's is a teaser to pique your curiosity a little!
